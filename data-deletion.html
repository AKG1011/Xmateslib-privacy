User Data Deletion Instructions

Xmateslib · Effective date: August 09, 2025

If you would like to delete your personal data associated with Xmateslib (for example, data obtained through Facebook Login), please follow the instructions below.

How to Request Deletion

Email us at: support@xmateslib.com

Use the subject line: Data Deletion Request - Xmateslib

Please include the following details so we can locate your account:

  • Your full name
  • The email address used for your Xmateslib account
  • (Optional) Any relevant account ID or profile link

What Happens Next

  • We will acknowledge your request within 7 days.
  • Upon verification, we will delete the personal data we hold that is associated with your account, unless we are required to retain certain data to comply with legal obligations.
  • Once deletion is complete, we will send you a confirmation email.

Scope of Deletion

Deletion generally covers personal identifiers (e.g., name, email) used for authentication. Aggregated or anonymized data that does not identify you may be retained for analytics and service integrity.

Contact

If you have questions about this process, contact us at support@xmateslib.com.
